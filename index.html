Smart, Safe, Simple.

Everything you need for a worry-free cooking experience.

Real-time Weight Monitoring

Our high-precision load cell accurately tracks your LPG level. Check the remaining gas percentage anytime, anywhere through our mobile app.

Instant Leak Detection

The integrated MQ-5 sensor continuously sniffs the air for gas leaks. If a leak is detected, a loud alarm sounds and you receive an instant notification on your phone.

Low-Level Alerts

Weighnix intelligently notifies you when your gas is running low, giving you plenty of time to order a refill before you run out in the middle of cooking.

Effortless Setup, Powerful Protection.

Get started with Weighnix in three simple steps.

STEP 1

Place & Power On

Simply place your standard LPG cylinder on the durable Weighnix base and plug it into a power source. That's it.

STEP 2

Connect to the App

Download the Weighnix app on your smartphone. The ESP32's built-in Wi-Fi makes connecting to your home network a breeze.

STEP 3

Monitor with Confidence

You're all set! Monitor your gas levels, track usage history, and receive critical safety alerts directly on your phone.

Under the Hood

Built with reliable, industry-standard components.

Controller: ESP32 Microcontroller
Connectivity: 2.4GHz Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 4.2
Weight Sensor: High-Precision Load Cell
Decoder: HX711 24-bit ADC
Gas Sensor: MQ-5 (LPG, Natural Gas)
Alarm: 85dB Piezo Buzzer
Material: High-Density ABS Polymer
Power: 5V DC Adapter
